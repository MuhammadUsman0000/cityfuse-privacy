Data Deletion Policy

Last Updated: January 3, 2026

At CityFuse, we respect your right to control your personal data. This policy explains how you can request deletion of your data from our systems.

1. Your Right to Delete Data

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data at any time. Upon receiving your request, we will permanently delete your account and all associated data from our systems.

2. What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, profile photo
  • Trip History: All past trip records and booking history
  • Payment Information: Saved payment methods and wallet transaction history
  • Favorites: Saved routes, scheduled trips, and price alerts
  • App Preferences: Settings, notification preferences, and customizations
  • Support History: Previous customer support conversations

3. Data Retention After Deletion

Note: Some data may be retained for legal and regulatory compliance:

  • Transaction records may be kept for up to 7 years for tax/legal purposes
  • Anonymized analytics data (not personally identifiable) may be retained
  • Data required for ongoing legal disputes or investigations
