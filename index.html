Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 3, 2026

Welcome to CityFuse ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the CityFuse mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number when you create an account
  • Payment Information: Payment method details for wallet top-ups and ticket purchases
  • Profile Data: Profile photo, saved addresses, favorite routes
  • Communication: Messages you send to our support team

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Location Data: GPS location for route planning, nearby transport options, and live tracking (only when app is in use)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, trip history, booking patterns
  • Log Data: App crashes, performance data, timestamps

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our transport booking services
  • Process payments and wallet transactions
  • Show real-time transport information and live tracking
  • Personalize your experience with route suggestions
  • Send service notifications (trip updates, payment confirmations)
  • Analyze app usage to improve our services
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Location Services

Important: CityFuse uses your location to provide core functionality including:

  • Finding nearby metro stations, bus stops, and cab services
  • Real-time trip tracking
  • Route planning and navigation
  • Fare calculation based on distance

You can disable location access in your device settings, but this will limit app functionality.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, analytics, crash reporting
  • Google Maps: Map display and route planning
  • Google AdMob: Display advertisements (non-personalized ads available)
  • Payment Processors: Secure payment processing for wallet transactions

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your data.

5. Advertising

CityFuse displays advertisements through Google AdMob. You may see:

  • Personalized Ads: Based on your interests and app usage (if you consent)
  • Non-Personalized Ads: Generic ads not based on your data

You can opt out of personalized advertising in the app settings or through your device's ad settings.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information:

  • With transport operators to complete your bookings
  • With payment processors to handle transactions
  • With analytics providers to improve our services
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect rights, safety, or property

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication with Firebase
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited employee access to personal data

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Trip history is kept for 2 years for your reference. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Disable location services
  • Opt out of personalized advertising

10. Children's Privacy

CityFuse is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
